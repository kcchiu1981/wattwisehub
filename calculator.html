How it works

kWh per day = (Watts × Hours) ÷ 1000

Monthly cost ≈ kWh/day × 30 × price

Yearly cost ≈ kWh/day × 365 × price

What to measure first

Space heaters, dehumidifiers, old fridges, aquarium heaters, gaming PCs, and dryers are common energy hogs.
